Howdy! After dabbling in some self-taught online courses for web development, I decided that I wanted to challenge myself and make the career move to become a full-stack web developer. I completed the Web Development Immersive course through General Assembly and learned that I love building beautiful, functional sites and web apps! This career switch has been one of the most challenging and rewarding things that I have ever done, along with attempting to play a perfect game in Phase 10 (which I've yet to do, but I haven't given up).

I always have and always will love sports, both playing and watching. After moving to Austin, I worked with RBI Austin and later joined the Run Free Texas team as a Team Leader for the duration of three programs and am deeply passionate about using sports as a way to help others. I currently serve on the Program Committee of the Run Free Texas Board. I also love being outdoors and am constantly looking for new places to hike and play outside with my wife, Kelly, and our dog, Indiana.

If you would like to reach out to me for anything web related or for a rousing game of Phase 10 (or any other game for that matter; I thrive on competition), please shoot me an email!
